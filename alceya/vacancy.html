Head of Engineering 100% (m/w/d)

Liesberg, Switzerland

About position

Acino is a Swiss pharmaceutical company, leader in advanced drug delivery technologies. We have a clear focus on selected emerging markets in the Middle East, Africa, the CIS Region, and Latin America, and we operate in some of the most dynamic countries of the world. We value courage, commitment, trust, and empathy and provide an environment that supports initiative and effort. We are proud to be action-oriented and open-minded, with a strong focus on quality and product availability, even in remote and hard to reach areas of the world.

We are constantly expanding our market position with a future-oriented international business strategy. Therefore, we are looking for a dedicated and motivated person to strengthen our Engineering team in Aesch and Liesberg as

Head of Engineering 100% (m/w/d)

In this role, you are reporting directly to our Site Head. Your main mission is to ensure, coordinate and organize all engineering activities at both Sites

Main Tasks:

  • Lead and Coach direct reports and enable them to fulfil their tasks within their Teams and functional responsibilities
  • Disciplinary and functional management of the whole Engineering Team (40 employee) including budget responsibility and year end processes
  • Responsible for bulk and packaging equipment investments as well as infrastructure facilities including feasibility studies, qualification activities and concept studies
  • Develop and put into action a Site Master Plan
  • Overall responsibility for CAPEX projects including budget and timelines
  • Experience in planning and executing a Site Consolidation

Your Profile:

  • Master degree in relevant field such as Chemical or Pharmaceutical Engineering or Mechanical Engineering or relevant other qualification gained in a previous role
  • At least 8 years of experience, ideally in a pharmaceutical or biotech manufacturing environment
  • Strong Leadership skills
  • Ability to work autonomously and to lead within a matrix organization
  • Proven and strong negotiation, communication, project management and influencing skills
  • Ability to work under pressure in a challenging, demanding and fast changing environment.
  • High resistance to pressure and the capability to handle short timelines and complexity
  • German mother tongue, fluent in English both written and spoken, French as an asset

We offer a very attractive working environment within a highly motivated team with excellent working conditions. You can expect the following benefits:

  • A job with a crisis-proof pharmaceutical company
  • An international working environment in a constantly growing company
  • A motivated, agile and service-oriented team
  • Attractive salary
  • Flexible working hours (possibility of home office)
  • Generous pension fund
  • Free parking facilities
  • Coffee, tea and fruit at your free disposal
  • Attractive additional benefits such as subsidized lunch or Fitpass

This is the opportunity to join a very dynamic organization, where decisions are taken fast and where you can actively participate in shaping our future. If this sounds exciting, we would love to hear more about you!
