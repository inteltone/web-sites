Работа у нас

With a clear focus on selected markets in the Middle East, Africa, the CIS Region, and Latin America, we are operating in some of the most dynamic countries of the world.

почему стоит работать в компании Алцея

We make and provide quality medicines having patients in mind. We provide relief for patients, a high quality choice for doctors, sustainable business for our partners and a good place to work for our employees.

With our 'hands-on' mentality, short communication channels and quick decision-making we deliver sustainable results for our customers, partners and employees.

Your career at Acino

We value courage, commitment, trust, and empathy and provide an environment that supports initiative and effort. The role you start with will evolve and change depending on your personality and determination.
