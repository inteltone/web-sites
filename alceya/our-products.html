Наши продукты

At Acino, we make high-quality medicines accessible to patients and healthcare professionals where and when it’s needed most. We develop, manufacture and internationally market innovative and well proven pharmaceuticals in selected high-growth markets.

Medicines for all

Over the years, we have successfully expanded our portfolio through in-licensing and acquisitions and are well positioned for growth. We work closely with our partners to bring effective and innovative drugs to the markets where we operate. Our world-class manufacturing capabilities and network are also leveraged by other pharma companies, making us their contract manufacturing and out-licensing partner of choice.

Pharmacovigilance

At Acino we take the safety of our patients very seriously. We know that side effects can happen, but it is not always possible to predict who will experience them. We continuously monitor the safety of our products. In case you experience a side effect after using an Acino product, please report it to us.
